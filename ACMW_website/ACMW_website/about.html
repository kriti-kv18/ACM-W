ACM-W: Supporting, celebrating, and advocating for Women in Computing

PCCOE ACM-W (Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering Association for Computing Machinery-Women) is dedicated to fostering the active involvement of students in various facets of the computing field. Through a diverse array of programs and services, the chapter is committed to enhancing the contributions of women in technology within our college community. The chapter exhibits a noteworthy trajectory of continuous improvement across various parameters. Each passing year witnesses a concerted effort to expand and evolve, guided by dedicated faculty sponsors, the energy of enthusiastic volunteers, and the active participation of students. The commitment to professional development is evident in the chapter's strategic initiatives and forward-looking approach. PCCOE ACM-W serves as a dynamic platform for students to engage comprehensively with the ever-evolving landscape of computing. This growth is facilitated by a collaborative environment, where faculty sponsors provide valuable guidance, volunteers contribute their passion and skills, and students actively partake in shaping the chapter's trajectory. The chapter's progress is indicative of its commitment to staying abreast of industry trends, technological advancements, and the evolving needs of the computing sector. PCCOE ACM-W's dedication to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for women in technology underscores its pivotal role in shaping the future of computing professionals within the college community.

ACM-W PCCoE

Established in 2019. This chapter has been established with an aim to support and motivate the women in computing, and thereby bring about gender equality in this field.
